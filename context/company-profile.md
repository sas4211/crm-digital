# FlowDesk — Company Profile

## What We Do
FlowDesk is a cloud-based **project management + lightweight CRM** platform for small and mid-market teams. We help teams track work, automate repetitive tasks, and keep customer deals moving — all in one place.

**Tagline:** *"Work flows when teams flow."*

## Founded
2021 — bootstrapped, now Series A ($8M raised, March 2024)

## Team
- 38 employees (12 engineering, 6 product, 4 customer success, rest GTM + ops)
- HQ: Austin, TX (remote-first)

## Customer Base
- ~1,200 paying customers
- ~8,500 active users
- Verticals: SaaS startups, marketing agencies, consulting firms, e-commerce ops teams

## Pricing Plans

| Plan | Price | Users | Key Limits |
|------|-------|-------|------------|
| **Free** | $0/mo | Up to 3 | 5 projects, 1GB storage, no automations |
| **Starter** | $29/mo | Up to 10 | Unlimited projects, 10GB, 50 automations/mo |
| **Pro** | $79/mo | Up to 50 | 100GB, unlimited automations, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | SSO, SLA, dedicated CSM, custom integrations |

Annual billing = 2 months free.

## Support Structure
- **Free plan:** Community forum + docs only (no direct support)
- **Starter:** Email support, 48-hour SLA
- **Pro:** Email + chat support, 12-hour SLA, priority queue
- **Enterprise:** Dedicated CSM, 4-hour SLA, phone support
